Below are some materials I would recommend to begin learning music theory, or materials I have used learn guitar

  • Absolute begginer baiscs (this channel is very good for a begginer wanting to learn chords and start playing songs)

    Learn barre chords as soon as possible!!! they open the whole kneck of the guitar to you.

  • Begginer music theory for guitar

Things I would strongly recommend learning

    Note names of each string
    How to make scales from any starting note (major: t-t-s-t-t-t-s, minor: t-s-t-t-s-t-t)
    How to play chords shapes up the kneck, i.e. the caged system (focusing on the E and A chord shapes alone can make a huge difference)
    Learning how chords are made (major: 4-note 3-note spacing, minor: 3-note 4-note spacing)
